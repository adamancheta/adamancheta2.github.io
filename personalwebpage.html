About Me

Hello! My name is Adam Ancheta, I am from Honolulu, HI. I am currently a second year at Northeastern University studying Data Science & Economics. This is my personal website that highlights the different achievements and updates of my personal and professional career. I hope all of you enjoy my website! Some of the clubs that I am involved in at Northeastern are the IISE Club, Case Institute, and Hawaii Club. Some of my hobbies outside the classroom are surfing, running, and tennis. When I think of choosing my future career, I have always been extremely interested in investment banking and consulting. I find consulting and investment banking interesting, because it involves maintaining client relationships and involves helping businesses restructure and improve. I am currently a sophomore studying Economics & Data Science at Northeastern University. Some of my experience includes leading a project with First Gen Empowerment Organization, a non-profit that teaches and helps underprivileged kids navigate the world of entrepreneurship. We created a market analysis presentation and created a layout for creating a First Gen Entrepreneurship Bootcamp. Additionally, I currently work at a local real estate firm, Boston Real Estate Collaborative as an Accounting Intern. Some of the day-to-day tasks as an intern are performing bank reconciliations and bookkeeping tasks. When dealing with bank reconciliations, it involves comparing transactions on the company's bank statement and matches with transactions inputted into Appfolio. Learning Appfolio, a property management accounting software, is very useful as it allows me to perform bookkeeping and ensure accuracy. This ensures that the accounting department manager will have the necessary documents and clean filing system to prepare for the tax season.
